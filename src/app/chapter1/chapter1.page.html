Chapter 1 | Shapes

It all started with graphic design. I was working for a non profit and designing for their campaigns. I would take work home and somewhere in the middle I would find myself fiddling around with designs that were just for me. Shapes and colors that represented what I was feeling at the time. I often found the process of design unlocking a part of myself that had been hidden. Other times the shapes and colors gave voice to what I was wrestling through. I felt as though these things I made were a mirror to gain perspective and see my own patterns; Wounds and childhood experiences that seemed to only produce negative consequences began to unravel and fall off my life. Through it all - I never intended that people would see my designs. Little did I know what would happen next! That season of creating taught me a lot. It’s okay to create out of pain. It’s equally okay to create out of growth & celebration. I also discovered the beauty of creating something just for myself. Without the need to share it; someone else’s approval could never truly validate who I am.
